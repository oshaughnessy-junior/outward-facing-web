UWM recently hosted the [23rd Midwest Relativity Meeting](http://www.gravity.phys.uwm.edu/conferences/mwrm2013/index.html), with a satellite Compact Objects Meeting With more than 70 participants over three days, talks covered everything from gravitational theory to observations and astrophysics of compact binaries, showcasing the breadth of modern gravitational physics.
