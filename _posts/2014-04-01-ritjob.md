I am excited to be joining the faculty at the Rochester Institute of Technology in the fall!

I will be a member of the [Center for Computational Relativity and Gravitation (CCRG)](http://ccrg.rit.edu/) in the [School of Mathematical Sciences (SMS)](http://www.rit.edu/~w-sms/academics.html) and affiliated with the [Astrophysical Sciences and Technology (AST)](http://www.rit.edu/cos/physics/) Program in the School of Physics and Astronomy. Both [SMS](http://www.rit.edu/~w-sms/academics.html) and AST provide students opportunities for graduate and [undergraduate](http://www.rit.edu/research/undergrad.php) research.
